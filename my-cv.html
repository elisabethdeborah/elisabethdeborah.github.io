My CV

I am a lawyer turned frontend developer, currently studying in my first year of frontend development at Handelsakademin in Gothenburg, Sweden. I will graduate in June of 2022.

I became interested in web design about five years ago, and started to get more serous about it about one and a half years ago. At that time I considered becoming a self-tought developer, while still working as a lawyer.

In August of 2020 I finally decided that I wanted to leave the legal field and get a degree in frontend developement. Right from the start I felt that this is the right thing for me, and I really enjoy learning more and more every day.
